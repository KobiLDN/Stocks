SPY AMEX:SPY
S&P 500 ETF. Tracks the 500 largest US public companies. The most widely watched gauge of overall US stock market health — when people say "the market is up/down," this is usually what they mean.
QQQ NASDAQ:QQQ
Nasdaq 100 ETF. Tracks 100 of the largest non-financial companies on the Nasdaq — heavily weighted toward tech and growth. A proxy for sentiment on AI, semiconductors, and Big Tech specifically.
VIX CAPITALCOM:VIX
The "Fear Index." Measures how much volatility options traders expect in the S&P 500 over the next 30 days. Low (below ~18) = calm markets; high (above ~25) = fear and uncertainty. Tends to spike when SPY/QQQ fall sharply.
